Cowboy Casserole

Description

This hearty cowboy casserole is comfort food at its best with a creamy filling and crispy tots.

Ingredients

  • bacon
  • yellow onion
  • red bell pepper
  • ground sirloin
  • garlic
  • cream of mushroom soup
  • milk
  • corn kernels

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) with a rack in the center position
  2. Heat a large cast-iron skillet over medium. Add bacon and cook, stirring often, until browned and crisp, about 7 minutes.
  3. Add onion and bell pepper to drippings in skillet
  4. Sprinkle top evenly with remaining 1 cup cheese and arrange tots in concentric circles over cheese layer
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until top is golden brown and beef mixture is bubbly
